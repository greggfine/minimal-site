Gregg Fine

makes the music that helps you build your brand. Hidden Tiger Music’s composer/producer Gregg Fine believes in the power of music to take picture to a new level, heighten emotions, and sometimes just provide a cool underscore. It’s about helping you achieve your vision. Sometimes that starts at the beginning of a project-pulling tracks and finding a direction-discovering musical solutions that create emotional impact and help tell a story. With a diligent and reliable work ethic, Hidden Tiger Music stands by you through the various life-stages of a project, ultimately delivering a unique, high-end, and often unexpected score.
